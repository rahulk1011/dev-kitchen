Oreo Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • 120gm Oreo Biscuits
  • 50ml Warm Milk
  • Chocolates (Dairy Milk or Any Dark Chocolate)
  • Chocolate Chips, Chocolate Syrup

Instructions

  • Crush the biscuits. And then add milk to make a thick paste.
  • Add chocolate chips & chocolate syrup, if you want.
  • Pour the mixture into small cups.
  • Add small chocolate pieces in the middle.
  • Bake for 10-15 minutes at 180Â°C.
  • Sprinkle powder sugar on top & serve.
