Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • 250gm Powdered Sugar
  • 250gm Butter
  • 3 spoons Baking Powder
  • 50gm Cocoa Powder
  • 300gm White Flour

Instructions

  • Melt the butter & mix it with sugar.
  • Add eggs to the mixture & mix it well.
  • Stir flour, cocoa powder & baking powder together.
  • Then add this to the egg mixture. Mix well.
  • Pre-heat oven for 5-10 minutes at 180°C.
  • Grease a baking tray & add the cake batter into it.
  • Bake for 30-40 minutes at 180°C.
  • Serve with chocolate syrup.
