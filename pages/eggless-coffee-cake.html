Eggless Coffee Cake

Ingredients

  • 5 spoon Coffee
  • 50gm Powdered Sugar
  • 50ml Milk
  • 100gm White Flour
  • 2 spoon baking powder
  • 25gm Butter

Instructions

  • Mix the coffee, sugar & milk in a bowl.
  • Add flour & baking powder into the mixture.
  • Now add butter & mix it well till the batter is smooth.
  • Pre-heat oven for 5 minutes at 180°C.
  • Grease a baking tray & put the batter into it.
  • Bake for 15-20 minutes at 180°C.
  • Serve with chocolate syrup on top.
