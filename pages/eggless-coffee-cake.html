Eggless Coffee Cake


Ingredients
Instructions
  • 1 Mix the coffee, sugar & milk in a bowl.
  • 2 Add flour & baking powder into the mixture.
  • 3 Now add butter & mix it well till the batter is smooth.
  • 4 Pre-heat oven for 5 minutes at 180°C.
  • 5 Grease a baking tray & put the batter into it.
  • 6 Bake for 15-20 minutes at 180°C.
  • 7 Serve with chocolate sauce on top.
