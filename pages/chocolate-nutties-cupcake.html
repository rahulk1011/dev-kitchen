Chocolate Nutties Cupcake


Ingredients
Instructions
  • 1 Grind 3 packets of Nutties & Marie biscuits in a mixer. Mix them with butter.
  • 2 Set the mixture in small cups or cake base & freeze it for 2 hours.
  • 3 In a bowl, melt the Dark chocolate & mix it with the fresh cream.
  • 4 We will add this mixture as the first layer on the base.
  • 5 In a separate bowl, whip the whipping cream & vanilla using a blender.
  • 6 Take out the chocolate base from the freezer & add the fresh cream mixture on top.
  • 7 Then add the whipped cream mixture on top of it.
  • 8 Freeze the cupcakes again for 3-4 hours.
  • 9 Garnish it with chopped nutties & chocolate chips.
  • 10 Serve cold.
